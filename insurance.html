Health Insurance

Health insurance is an insurance that covers the whole or a part of the risk of a person incurring medical expenses, spreading the risk over numerous persons.
The benefit is administered by a central organization such as a government agency, private business, or not-for-profit entity.

categories of health insurance

There are generally two categories of health insurances, they are

  • Long term Insurances
  • Short term Insurances

1. Long term Insurance

long term health insurance has a tenure of 2 to 3 years. long term health insurance provides comprehensive coverage.

2. Short term Insurance

short term health insurance is for a period of up to 12 months. short term health insurance covers major expenses against medical emergencies for a shorter duration.
